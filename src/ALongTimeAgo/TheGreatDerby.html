2021 | UNSTABLE

Made By: Alex Su, Micha Rand

A ridiculous premise, but it starts to all make sense once you sit with it a while. I don't remember why we settled on a typing mechanic, but the choice of unusual bottom row keys unintentionally produced quite a challenging experience.

Play on itch.io

I remember development was quite smooth, having to balance it with schoolwork and the limit of 72 hours. The music and sounds by Micha were especially brilliant.

The individual parts may not stand on their own, but they really came together quite harmoniously. There's genuine urgency when you hear the hooves growing louder while wrangling with the fireworks. All in all this turned out to be very charming game, I still like it a lot.
